About us


Our team is a group of 10 teenagers who came together under a program called MEET in order to help other teenagers. We envision a reality where teens can access mental health care easily without going through a complicated process and by that improving their mental health. Our mission is to provide easily accessible mental health care service through a website. Our startup operates with the help of post-grad psychology students to help teenagers receive the best mental aid possible when facing difficult mentally situations.
